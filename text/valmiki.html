Valmiki

Oswald Foundation was founded with the development of Valmiki, which is a plug-in for Chromium-based web browsers like Google Chrome and Opera that changes the look and feel of the entire internet based on your preferences.

Valmiki is especially helpful for people with dyslexia, because it comes built-in with an option that adjusts the color-contrast ratio and typography to best suit the needs of someone with a learning disorder, based on previous research conducted by the W3C and the British Dyslexia Association.
